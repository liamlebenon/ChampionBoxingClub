Champion Boxing Club

We Are the Home of Champions

We offer the highest quality training in the sport of boxing. We have been training champions at our gyms for over 50 years. Our fighters have travelled world-wide to bring their titles home, showing the world how great they are.

Veteran Trainers

We are proud to say that we have the best trainers in South Africa, with a combined training experience of 350 years. Our trainers specialise in different areas to ensure that you get the best experience possible.
